Heading to Paris-Orly Airport (ORY)? Here’s how to make it hassle-free:

  • Metro Line 14
    • Board at Châtelet-Les Halles directly to Aéroport d’Orly (~25 min)
    • Airport fare ~€11.50;
  • RER B + Orlyval
    • RER B to Antony, then Orlyval shuttle to terminals
    • Around 35 min; fare ~€12
  • OrlyBus
    • Depart Denfert-Rochereau to Orly (~30–40 min)
    • Fare ~€11–12
  • Metro 7 + Tram T7
    • Metro 7 to Villejuif–Louis Aragon, then Tram T7 to the airport via Orly Val
    • Budget-friendly; ~60 min total; fare ~€3.80
  • Taxi / Pre-booked Transfer
    • Flat fares: ~€32 (Left Bank), ~€37 (Right Bank)
    • Quickest door-to-door (~30 min)

Pro tip: If luggage or evening departure, consider a taxi or pre-booked ride for comfort and reliability.
