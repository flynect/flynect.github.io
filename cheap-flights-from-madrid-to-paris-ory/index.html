Cheap Flights from Madrid to Paris-ORY

Discover the cheapest round-trip fares from Madrid to Paris-ORY, all sourced directly from airlines — no third-party markups, no hidden fees.

Traveling from Paris-Orly Airport (ORY) to downtown Paris has never been easier. Your best options:

  • Metro Line 14 (new extension)
    • Direct service from Aéroport d’Orly to Châtelet-Les Halles in ~25 min
    • Fare: approx. €11.50 with airport ticket or Navigo; Navigo pass holders may not pay extra :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
  • Orlyval + RER B
    • Take Orlyval to Antony, then RER B to central Paris (e.g., Châtelet, Gare du Nord)
    • Journey ~35 min, fare around €12
  • OrlyBus
    • Direct shuttle to Denfert-Rochereau (~30–40 min)
    • Cost: ~€11–12
  • Tram T7 + Metro 7
    • Take Tram T7 from Orly to Villejuif–Louis Aragon, then Metro 7 into Paris
    • Affordable, total time ~60 min, fare ~€1.90 + €1.90
  • Taxi / Private Transfer
    • Flat rate: ~€32 to Left Bank, ~€37 to Right Bank
    • Duration: ~30–40 min depending on traffic

Tip: For speed and convenience, Metro 14 is outstanding. On a budget? Tram T7 is the cheapest option.
