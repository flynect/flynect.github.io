Cheap Flights from Dublin to Barcelona

Discover the cheapest round-trip fares from Dublin to Barcelona, all sourced directly from airlines — no third-party markups, no hidden fees.

Barcelona El Prat Airport (BCN) is located about 15 km from the city center, and getting there is quick and easy with several transport options.

  • Aerobús – Direct service to Plaça de Catalunya in about 35 minutes.
    Runs every 5–10 minutes. Tickets cost around €6 one-way.

  • Train (R2 Nord) – Connects Terminal 2 to Sants, Passeig de Gràcia, and El Clot stations.
    Travel time: ~25 minutes. Cost: €5.
    Note: No train from Terminal 1 — use the free shuttle bus to T2.

  • Metro (L9 Sud) – From both terminals to Zona Universitària, then transfer to other lines.
    Travel time: 45–50 minutes. Special airport ticket: €5.50.

  • Taxi – Fastest option, 20–30 minutes depending on traffic.
    Cost: €30–€35.

  • Ride-hailing services – Apps like Free Now or Cabify are available.

  • Private transfers – Pre-booked comfort at a fixed price.

With so many options, you can choose the balance of speed, price, and comfort that suits your trip.
