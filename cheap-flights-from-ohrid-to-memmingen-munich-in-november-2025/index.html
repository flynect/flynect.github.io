Landed at Memmingen Airport (FMM)? Here’s how to reach Munich efficiently:

  • Express Bus (FlixBus / Allgäu-Airport-Express) – Direct to Munich Central (Hbf): ~1 h 20 min; tickets from €5–12, average €11–17.
  • Airport City Shuttle + DB Train – Shuttle to Memmingen station (~10 min), then train to Munich (~1 h 40 min total).
  • Taxi / Private Transfer – Fastest (~1 h 15 min door-to-door) but expensive (~€250 fixed fare).

Tip: For value and convenience, express buses are your best bet—book early for lowest fares.
