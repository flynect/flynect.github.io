Heading to Elefthérios Venizélos from Athens center? Here’s how:

  • Metro — Line 3 Blue
    • Runs from Syntagma/Monastiraki to the Airport, ~40 minutes.
    • One-way airport ticket: €9, return €16. Includes 90-minute transfer validity.
  • Express Bus (X95 etc.)
    • X95 departs city center (Syntagma) every 20–30 minutes; journey ~60 minutes, fare ~€5.50.
  • Suburban Railway to Larissis + change
    • Proastiakos trains to the airport run every 15–25 mins, ~45-minute ride. Connects with metro/trains.
  • Taxi (Flat Rate)
    • Day: €40; night: €55 (includes all surcharges).
  • Private Transfer / Pre-booked Taxi
    • Typically €45 day / €55 night for cars, €82 for minivan.

Pro Tip: For luggage-heavy travelers or off-hours, a pre-booked private transfer offers peace of mind and predictable pricing.
