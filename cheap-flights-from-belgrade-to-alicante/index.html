Cheap Flights from Belgrade to Alicante

Discover the cheapest round-trip fares from Belgrade to Alicante, all sourced directly from airlines — no third-party markups, no hidden fees.

Alicante airport (ALC) lies ~9 km south of the city. Reliable public options:

  • Bus — Line C-6 (Airport Shuttle)
    • Direct service to Alicante city (Renfe station, Plaza Puerta del Mar).
    • Frequency: every 20 min daytime; hourly late night.
    • Fare: ≈ €3.85–€4 (cash/contactless); cheaper multi-trip cards available.
  • Taxi
    • Metered taxis available outside arrivals. Typical trip to center: ≈ €20–€25 depending on traffic and time. (Cash/contactless accepted).
  • Car rental
    • Several agencies at the terminal; drive time to the city ~15–20 min.

Tip: C-6 is the most budget-friendly direct link. At night the frequency drops — if you travel late check schedules or allow extra time for a taxi.
