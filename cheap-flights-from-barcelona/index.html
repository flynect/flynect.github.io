Cheap Flights from Barcelona

Discover direct flights from Barcelona to 50+ European and World hubs. Compare real-time fares and book instantly — no additional fees!

Getting from Barcelona city center to El Prat Airport (BCN) is simple, with multiple transport options to fit any budget or schedule.

  • Aerobús – Departs from Plaça de Catalunya, with stops at Sepúlveda–Urgell and Plaça Espanya.
    Runs every 5–10 minutes. Travel time: ~35 minutes. Cost: €6.

  • Train (R2 Nord) – From Passeig de Gràcia and Sants stations to Terminal 2.
    Travel time: ~25 minutes. Cost: €5.
    Note: For Terminal 1, take the free shuttle bus from T2.

  • Metro (L9 Sud) – Connects both terminals to Zona Universitària for transfers to other lines.
    Travel time: 45–50 minutes. Special airport ticket: €5.50.

  • Taxi – Direct and fast, 20–30 minutes depending on traffic.
    Cost: €30–€35.

  • Ride-hailing services – Free Now, Cabify, and similar apps available.

  • Private transfers – Pre-booked rides with fixed pricing.

Whether you value speed, comfort, or savings, Barcelona offers a smooth journey from the heart of the city to the airport.
