Cheap Flights from London-Gatwick to Prague in January 2026

Discover the cheapest round-trip fares from London-Gatwick to Prague, all sourced directly from airlines — no third-party markups, no hidden fees.

Arriving at Václav Havel Airport Prague (PRG)? Here are your top transport choices:

  • Public Bus + Metro (Bus 59 or 100)
    • Bus 59 goes to Veleslavín Metro Line A (Green); Bus 100 goes to Zličín Metro Line B (Yellow).
    • Buses run every few minutes; total journey to city center in ~30 minutes. Fare: ~40 CZK for 90-minute ticket.
  • Airport Express Bus (AE)
    • Direct service to Prague Main Railway Station (~40 min); ticket sold on board or in arrivals.
    • If purchased with Czech Railways fare, no extra ticket needed.
  • Shuttle or Private Transfer
    • Expect to pay ~€17 for shared shuttle or ~€33 for private pre-booked ride, with quieter, hotel-door convenience.
  • Taxi / Ride-hailing (Uber, Bolt)
    • Estimated cost: 600–800 CZK; Uber is the official airport taxi. Avoid unlicensed cabs.

Tip: For budget-friendly and reliable access, the public bus + metro combo is best. Prefer comfort? Airport Express or private transfer deliver hassle-free rides.
