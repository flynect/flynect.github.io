Cheap Flights from Bergamo-Milan to Stockholm in October 2025

Discover the cheapest round-trip fares from Bergamo-Milan to Stockholm, all sourced directly from airlines — no third-party markups, no hidden fees.

Stockholm Arlanda (ARN) sits about 40 km north of central Stockholm. Here are the reliable travel options:

  • Arlanda Express train
    • Fastest: 18 minutes to Stockholm Central.
    • Frequency: every 10–15 minutes during the day, up to every 12 min in rush hour.
    • Fare: approximately 340 SEK (~€30) one way (discounts available for groups, students, seniors).
  • Flygbussarna Airport Coach
    • Departs every 15–30 minutes to Cityterminalen next to the Central Station.
    • Travel time: 35–45 minutes.
    • Fare: usually around ~159 SEK (varies slightly).
  • Commuter (SL) train
    • Runs twice hourly from Arlanda Central via SkyCity.
    • Journey to the city: ~43 minutes.
    • Cheaper, but requires SL ticket and sometimes zone add-ons.
  • Taxi / Ride-hail
    • Travel time: ~40 minutes depending on traffic.
    • Fares vary; taxis not regulated — book through reputable operators like Taxi Stockholm or 020.

Traveler Insight: As one user noted, you can “pay 125 SEK for a Flygbuss and 320 SEK for Arlanda Express, or just 39 SEK using an SL ticket if you don’t mind slower service.”
