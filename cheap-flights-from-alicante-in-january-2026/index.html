Traveling from central Alicante to the airport:

  • C-6 Airport Bus
    • Catch the bus from main city stops (Renfe station, Puerta del Mar). Runs every ~20 minutes (less frequent at night). Fare ≈ €3.85–€4; buy on board or pay contactless.
  • Taxi
    • Short meter ride: ≈ €20–€25 to the terminal; allow extra time at peak hours.
  • Driving / Car Return
    • Follow A-7/AP-7 signage; car parks and rental return zones at the airport.

Pro tip: If you have early flights, plan for the reduced night frequency of C-6 or pre-book a taxi to avoid delays.
