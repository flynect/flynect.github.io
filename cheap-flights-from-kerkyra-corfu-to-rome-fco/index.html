Cheap Flights from Kerkyra-Corfu to Rome-FCO

Discover the cheapest round-trip fares from Kerkyra-Corfu to Rome-FCO, all sourced directly from airlines — no third-party markups, no hidden fees.

Landing at Leonardo da Vinci–Fiumicino Airport (FCO)? Choose your match:

  • Leonardo Express Train
    • Non-stop to Roma Termini (~32 min); runs every 15 min
    • Fare: ~€14
  • Regional FL1 Train
    • Stops at Ostiense, Tiburtina, Trastevere (~26–45 min)
    • Fare: ~€8
  • Taxi (Fixed Rate)
    • Flat rate: €100–120 to center; 40–60 min depending on traffic
  • Shuttle Buses (Terravision, SITbus, TAM bus)
    • To Termini, starting at ~€6.50–€7; varied services

Tip: For speed and simplicity, the Leonardo Express is the best. Budget-conscious? FL1 or shuttle buses are excellent value. Taxis offer convenience, but at a higher price.
