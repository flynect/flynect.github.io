Cheap Flights from Cluj-napoca to Malaga

Discover the cheapest round-trip fares from Cluj-napoca to Malaga, all sourced directly from airlines — no third-party markups, no hidden fees.

Málaga airport (AGP) sits ~8 km west of the historic center. Fast, cheap options:

  • Train — Cercanías C-1
    • Direct trains run from Terminal 3 to Málaga Centro-Alameda / María Zambrano.
    • Journey: ~12 minutes.
    • Fare: €1.80–€3.60 depending on zones; buy at machines or Renfe counters.
  • Airport bus — Line A (A Express)
    • Runs every ~20–30 minutes between the terminal and the city center.
    • Journey: ~15–25 minutes; fare typically €3–€4.5 (check Avanza timetables).
  • Taxi / Ride-hail
    • Available 24/7 outside arrivals; daytime to city centre ≈ €20–€30 (fixed meter fare may vary with new surcharges). Booked transfers cost more.
  • Car rental
    • Major agencies at the terminal; drive ~15–30 minutes depending on traffic.

Tip: The C-1 train is the fastest low-cost option when you’re luggage-light; allow extra time for taxi at peak hours due to recent tariff updates.
