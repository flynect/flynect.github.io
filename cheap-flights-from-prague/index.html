Cheap Flights from Prague

Discover direct flights from Prague to 11+ European and World hubs. Compare real-time fares and book instantly — no additional fees!

Heading to Václav Havel Airport Prague (PRG)? Use these convenient options:

  • Metro + Bus (Line A or B + Bus 59/100)
    • Take Metro Line A to Veleslavín, then Bus 59 to the airport—or Line B to Zličín, then Bus 100.
    • Fast (total ~30 min); fare = regular PID ticket (~40 CZK).
  • Airport Express (AE) Bus
    • Leaves from Prague Main Railway Station to the airport (~40 min); include in rail ticket—no separate fare.
    • Convenient if departing from the main station.
  • Private Shuttle / Transfer
    • Pre-book a shared (~€17) or private (~€33) transfer—excellent for rainy or early/late travel.
  • Taxi / Uber / Bolt
    • Fare: around 600–800 CZK. Uber is the official app-based airport service. Verify driver identity.

Pro tip: Metro + bus offers quick and cheap access. With heavy luggage or late-night departures, a private ride or officially booked transfer gives peace of mind.
