Cheap Flights from Dublin to Madrid

Discover the cheapest round-trip fares from Dublin to Madrid, all sourced directly from airlines — no third-party markups, no hidden fees.

Arriving at Adolfo Suárez Madrid–Barajas (MAD)? Here are your best transit options:

  • Metro Line 8
    • Connects Terminals (T2–T4) to Nuevos Ministerios (~15–20 min); transfers available across Madrid
  • Cercanías (C-1 Line)
    • From Terminal 4 to Atocha or Chamartín — great for train connections
  • Taxi
    • Official white taxis with red stripe; flat-rate zones (e.g. airport to city)
  • Ride-hailing (Bolt)
    • Designated pickup areas and flat fares since 2024

Tip: Metro Line 8 is fast and frequent. For train travelers, Cercanías C-1 gives direct access to major rail junctions. Taxis or Bolt offer hassle-free, door-to-door service.
