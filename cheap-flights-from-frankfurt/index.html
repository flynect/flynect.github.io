Cheap Flights from Frankfurt

Discover direct flights from Frankfurt to 1+ European and World hubs. Compare real-time fares and book instantly — no additional fees!

Leaving Frankfurt for FRA? These are your top transport choices:

  • S-Bahn S8 or S9 – Depart from Hauptwache, Hauptbahnhof, or other central stations; reach the airport in ~10–12 minutes. €5 zone ticket includes airport.
  • Regional Express (RE2, RE3, RE59) – Similar route, fewer stops; good during rush hours.
  • Bus Lines (RMV) – Services like 61, 62, X58 connect central areas to the airport terminals; fares and routes vary.
  • Taxi / Ride-hailing – Trip time ~10–15 minutes; fare around €30–40.
  • Car Rental Drop-off – Airport offers numerous drop-off points near terminals; plan for extra traffic during peak times.

Pro tip: Allow 5–10 extra minutes during peak hours when using road or bus routes. For reliability and simplicity, S-Bahn is king.
