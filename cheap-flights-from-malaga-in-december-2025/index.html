Cheap Flights from Malaga in December 2025

Discover direct flights from Malaga to 24+ European and World hubs. Compare real-time fares and book instantly — no additional fees!

Heading to AGP from downtown Málaga — choose depending on luggage and schedule:

  • Cercanías C-1 (train)
    • Board at María Zambrano or Málaga Centro-Alameda for a ~12-minute ride to Terminal 3.
    • Fare: €1.80–€3.60. Machines and kiosks available.
  • Airport Bus (A Express / Avanza)
    • Frequent departures; travel time ~15–30 minutes. Keep an eye on timetables—seasonal changes possible. Fare around €3–€5.
  • Taxi / Transfer
    • Metered taxis: expect €20–€30 to central areas (higher at night/with surcharges). Pre-booked private transfers offer fixed pricing.
  • Drive & Return Rental
    • Follow signs to terminal; rental returns well signposted.

Pro tip: If you fly early morning, check the first C-1 timetable; otherwise opt for a short taxi (allow 30–45 min during rush hours).
