Cheap Flights from Rome-FCO to Amsterdam in October 2025

Discover the cheapest round-trip fares from Rome-FCO to Amsterdam, all sourced directly from airlines — no third-party markups, no hidden fees.

Arriving at Amsterdam Airport Schiphol (AMS)? Here are top ways to reach downtown efficiently:

  • NS Train
    • Frequent trains run directly from Schiphol Station (beneath the terminal) to Amsterdam Centraal.
    • Journey: ~17 min; fare: ~€4.90 one-way; operates 24/7 with early and late options.
  • Amsterdam Airport Express Bus (Connexxion 397)
    • Departs from outside Schiphol Plaza to stops near Museumplein, Leidseplein.
    • Duration: ~30 min; fare: €6.50 one-way or €11.75 return.
  • Taxi / App-Based Ride (Uber, Bolt, FREENOW)
    • Official taxi stands available; app pickups at designated zones.
    • Typical fare: €35–55; beware of unlicensed drivers soliciting inside terminal.
  • Pre-Booked Shuttle
    • Fixed-price, door-to-door service. Often cheaper and more predictable than a regular taxi.

Tip: Trains are the fastest and most cost-effective. For hotel stops near Museumplein or Leidseplein, the Airport Express Bus is a direct and easy alternative.
