Cheap Flights from London-Gatwick to Paris-CDG

Discover the cheapest round-trip fares from London-Gatwick to Paris-CDG, all sourced directly from airlines — no third-party markups, no hidden fees.

Traveling from Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG) to central Paris? Consider these reliable options:

  • RER B Train
    • Runs from Terminals 1 & 2 to Châtelet-Les Halles, Gare du Nord (~35–45 min)
    • Fare: ~€13
  • Roissybus Shuttle
    • Direct to Opera (~60 min), departing every 15-20 min
    • Cost: ~€13
  • Official Taxi (Fixed Fare)
    • Flat rate: ~€56 (Right Bank) or ~€65 (Left Bank), 45–60 min
    • Always queue at official stands to avoid scams.
  • Shared or Private Shuttles
    • Shared: ~€43 per person; Private: €70-80 per vehicle—door-to-door service in 45–75 min

Tip: The RER B train is fastest and budget-friendly; for comfort with luggage or night arrivals, opt for a shuttle or official taxi.
