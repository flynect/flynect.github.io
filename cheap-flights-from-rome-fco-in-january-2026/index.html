Heading to Fiumicino (FCO) from Rome’s center? Compare your options:

  • Leonardo Express Train
    • Direct to FCO from Termini (~32 min), ~€14

    • From Ostiense, Tiburtina, etc.; fare ~€8
  • Taxi (Flat Fare)
    • Around €100–120; door-to-terminal in ~40–60 min

    • Terravision, SITbus, TAMBUS to FCO (~€6.50–€7)
