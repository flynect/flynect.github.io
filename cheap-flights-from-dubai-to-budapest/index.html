How to get from Budapest Airport (BUD) to Budapest city center:

  • By bus: 100E Airport Express connects to Deák Ferenc tér in 35–40 minutes.

  • By train: Bus 200E to Ferihegy station, then train to Nyugati station.

  • By taxi: ~30 minutes, ~€25.
