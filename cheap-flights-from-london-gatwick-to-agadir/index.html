Cheap Flights from London-Gatwick to Agadir

Discover the cheapest round-trip fares from London-Gatwick to Agadir, all sourced directly from airlines — no third-party markups, no hidden fees.

Travel from Agadir Al Massira Airport to downtown Agadir is easy with several reliable options:

  • ALSA Airport Bus (Line L-AE)
    • Departs ~every 60 minutes to “La Vallée des Oiseaux” via Inezgane.
    • Fare: 50 MAD one-way; 80 MAD return.
  • Official Taxis
    • Available 24/7 at arrivals.
    • Day rate: ~150 MAD; after 19:00, up to 200 MAD.
    • Travelers report fares around 200 MAD (~20 €) fixed.
  • Shared or Collective Taxis & Bus + Collective Mix
    • Shared “collective taxis” may cost ~10 MAD per person, though negotiation is needed.
    • Other public buses to Inzegane cost ~4 MAD, then a collective taxi to downtown may add 6 MAD.

Tip: Negotiate prices upfront—some users report saving by offering ~20 MAD for short rides, even when drivers quoted more.
